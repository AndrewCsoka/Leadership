# Effective Meetings

## General meeting protocol
Add this to all meeting invites please!

1. This is a [technical|planning|whatever] meeting
1. This meeting ends at ___
1. At ___ we will stop to agree next steps and wrap up
1. Outcomes we want are: ___
1. The agenda is: ___
1. Feel free to leave this meeting if:___
1. Does anyone have different expectations?

## Vendor meeting protocol
In an effort to improve efficiency of meetings with vendors we have the following advice

1. Ahead of time, ask the vendor:
    1. Who will be in the meeting?
    1. What is their role?
    1. If the vendor is leading the meeting, what is the agenda for the meeting?
1. Assign a scribe from our team
    1. this person should focus on recording notes from the meeting
    1. the notes should be posted to the group for review afterwards
1. Ensure the meeting starts with the agenda being presented
